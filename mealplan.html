Meal Planner Flask App

Summary

A work in progress Python Flask web app to meal plan for the coming week, track recipes, maintain fridge necessities and more!

Features

Login Page

  • Create an account to store your meal planner and get planning!

Meal Planner

  • Calendar set up for the coming week to plan out meal ideas and assist in making a grocery list.
  • Drop down selector for meal time and day of the week to add your desired meal to the planner.

Grocery List

  • Add grocery items as you need them or remove as you buy!
  • List accessable from any device.

Recipe List

  • List of recipes for inspiration of your meals, as well as the ability to add new recipes.
  • The original recipes were web scraped from What's in the Pan? using Beautiful Soup.

Technologies

Python's Flask, sqlite3, beautiful soup
